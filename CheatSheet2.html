HTML

1.) Components of HTML element:

Start-Tag, End-Tag, element content,

body - main part; the code for the page.

Begins with an smaller than sign and ends with an greather than sign.

3.) Attributes:

They are similar to parametres in programming, but they can also be part of tags.

correct declaration for HTML5: \1 !DOCTYPE html \g.
